A tribute: The Spice Girls

Some history/bio: The Spice Girls consist of 5 members: Scary (Mel B), Ginger (Geri Halliwell), Baby (Emma Bunton), Sporty (Mel C), and Posh (Victoria Beckham). The band originated from the UK and all members are native to the UK. The girls formed in 1994 and eventually signed to Virgin Records. From then, their success lead to a multi-million dollar merchandise industry and set the tone for the pop music genre for the remainder of the decade. Why do I admire the Spice Girls? Because they remind me of some of the best times of my life! They are a symbol of the 90s.

Did you know?

  • Their 1st Number One single was WANNABE, and their debut! Released July 8th, 1996
  • Their 1998 Spiceworld Tour was the highest grossing girl group tour of the 90s, and is the highest grossing girl group of all time
  • After the year 2000, the band took a break. And in 2007/08, started a reunion world tour. To again reunite for the London 2012 Olympics.
  • Most recently, four of the members (Scary, Sporty, Ginger and Baby) reunited once more for a solely UK exclusive reunion tour.
  • The 2019 Spice World Tour grossed $72.8 million dollars, making them the highest grossing girl group to finish out the decade!
